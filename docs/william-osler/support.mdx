## IntelAGENT Technical Support

For questions about the IntelAGENT platform, submitting claims, or troubleshooting technical issues, contact IntelAGENT directly.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Email" icon="envelope">
    Send an email to [agent@intelagent.ca](mailto:agent@intelagent.ca) and we will respond as soon as possible.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Phone" icon="phone">
    Call us at [647-812-6678](tel:6478126678) during business hours (Monday to Friday, 9 AM – 5 PM ET).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### IntelAGENT can help with:
- Trouble logging in or accessing your account
- Issues submitting or editing claims
- Missing billing codes or diagnostic codes
- Questions about claim statuses
- Technical issues with the mobile app or web portal
- General questions about the billing platform

## William Osler — Payment & Account Inquiries

For questions about **payment acceptance**, **bank account details**, or other account-related matters, contact William Osler's Medical Staffing Office directly.

<Warning>
  IntelAGENT cannot update your banking information. Changes to your bank account must be made through William Osler.
</Warning>

### William Osler can help with:
- Payment acceptance and remittance inquiries
- Updating your banking details
- Account setup and onboarding
- Questions about patient eligibility or insurance status
