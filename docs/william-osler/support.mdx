### IntelAGENT can help with:
- Trouble logging in or accessing your account
- Issues submitting or editing claims
- Missing billing codes or diagnostic codes
- Questions about claim statuses
- Technical issues with the mobile app or web portal
- General questions about the billing platform

## Managing your bank account

You can add or update the bank account for your reimbursements yourself, on the web portal or the mobile app — see [Updating Your Bank Account](/docs/william-osler/bank-account). Saving new details triggers a $0.01 test deposit that you confirm to activate payments.

## William Osler — Payment & Account Inquiries

For questions about **payment acceptance** or other account-related matters, contact William Osler's Medical Staffing Office directly.

### William Osler can help with:
- Payment acceptance and remittance inquiries
- Account setup and enrollment in the reimbursement program
- Questions about patient eligibility or insurance status
