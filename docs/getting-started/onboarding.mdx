## Overview

After you create your account you get **immediate access to explore the platform** — but before you can **submit claims** there are a few steps to complete. IntelAGENT guides you through them in the **onboarding wizard**.

You reach the wizard right after signing up, and you can return to it any time from the user menu → **User Onboarding**. A red dot stays on that menu item until everything is done.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/01-choose-program.png" alt="The onboarding wizard showing a numbered step list on the left and the Choose your billing program(s) step open on the right" />
</Frame>

<Note>
You can leave the wizard at any point with **Skip for now** and pick up where you left off later — your progress is saved.
</Note>

## Create your account

Sign up at [app.intelagent.ca/register](https://app.intelagent.ca/register). Enter your name, email, and a password (at least 8 characters), then click **Continue** — you'll go straight into the onboarding wizard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/00-register.png" alt="The IntelAGENT Register page with First Name, Last Name, Email address, Password, College number, and Billing number fields and a Continue button" />
</Frame>

<Info>
**William Osler physicians:** register with your **`@williamoslerhs.ca` email address**. That's what unlocks the **William Osler reimbursement** option in the next step. If you sign up with a different email, that option is disabled — contact the Medical Affairs office to be set up.
</Info>

## Step 1 — Choose your billing program(s)

Select how you bill. The two programs are **independent** — you can enable one or both:

- **OHIP billing** — bill OHIP directly. Requires a subscription and MC EDT designation.
- **William Osler reimbursement** — get reimbursed by EFT. Requires your bank account details; no credit card or designation needed. This option is only available if you registered with a `@williamoslerhs.ca` email.

Make your selection and click **Confirm & continue**. The remaining steps update to match the programs you chose.

<Info>
William Osler reimbursement is a self-service option for William Osler physicians. If you don't have a William Osler email, that option is disabled — contact the Medical Affairs office to be set up.
</Info>

## OHIP billing

If you enabled **OHIP billing**, complete these steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Subscribe to a plan">
    Choose the plan that fits your practice and add a payment method. Your card isn't charged during the free trial (see below).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/02-subscription.png" alt="The Subscribe to a plan step showing the Standard and Premium plan options" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your MC EDT designation">
    Authorize IntelAGENT to submit claims on your behalf by designating us as your EDT agent through GO Secure. The step lists every instruction inline; for the full walkthrough see the [MC EDT Designation](/docs/getting-started/designation) guide.

    When you've finished granting permissions, click **Confirm validation**. IntelAGENT checks with the Ministry that the designation is live. If it isn't confirmed yet, finish granting the MC EDT permissions and try again.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/03-designation.png" alt="The MC EDT designation step listing the seven GO Secure steps and a Confirm validation button" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activation & 90-day free trial">
    Once you're **subscribed** and your **designation is confirmed**, your account is activated automatically and your **90-day free trial** begins — no charge until it ends. You can now submit claims.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/08-activated-banner.png" alt="A green banner reading You're activated — ready to bill, with a note that the 90-day free trial is active" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Until your account is activated, any claims you create are saved as drafts and held — they are only submitted to OHIP once you're active.
</Note>

## William Osler reimbursement

If you enabled **William Osler reimbursement**, add the bank account your reimbursements are deposited to and confirm the test deposit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your bank account">
    Enter your institution, transit, and account number. When you save, we send a **$0.01 test deposit** to the account (within 30 days).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/05-bank-account.png" alt="The Add your bank account step with institution, transit, and account number fields" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your $0.01 test deposit">
    Once the test deposit appears on your statement, turn on **Bank account verified**. We don't send real payments until this is on — and you can turn it off any time (for example, if your banking details change).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/docs/web-app/onboarding/06-confirm-deposit.png" alt="The Confirm your $0.01 test deposit step with a Bank account verified toggle" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can update your bank account and toggle verification any time from **Settings** — see [Updating Your Bank Account](/docs/william-osler/bank-account).

## Tracking your progress

The left-hand step list shows the status of every step — completed steps are marked, and the current step is highlighted. Click any step to jump to it. When everything is done, the **User Onboarding** reminder in the user menu clears.
