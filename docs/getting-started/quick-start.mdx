## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A valid GO Secure account for EDT access
- Your OHIP billing number
- IntelAGENT designated as your agent (see [Designation Guide](/docs/getting-started/designation))

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [app.intelagent.ca/register](https://app.intelagent.ca/register)
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Select your billing plan (Free trial available)
4. Complete your profile information

## Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

After logging in, configure your billing profile:

1. Navigate to **Settings**
2. Enter your **physician information** (name, billing number)
3. Add your **facilities/locations**
4. Configure your **MOH group** and specialty

## Step 3: Submit Your First Claim

### Using the Web App

1. Click **New Claim** from the dashboard
2. Enter patient information (OHIP health card number)
3. Select the service date and facility
4. Add billing codes and diagnostic codes
5. Review the calculated fees
6. Click **Submit**

### Using the Mobile App

1. Open the app and tap the **Claims** tab
2. Enter or scan the patient's health card
3. Fill in the claim details
4. Optionally attach documents using the camera
5. Tap **Submit**

## Step 4: Track Your Claims

View submitted claims in the **Records** section (mobile) or **Claims** page (web). Filter by status:

- **Draft** - Saved but not submitted
- **Submitted** - Sent to OHIP for processing
- **Partial** - Partially paid
- **Rejected** - Requires attention
