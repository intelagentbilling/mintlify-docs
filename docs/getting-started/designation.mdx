## Overview

Before IntelAGENT can submit claims on your behalf, you must designate it as your agent through the Ministry of Health's EDT (Electronic Data Transfer) system.

<Info>
This process typically takes 1-2 business days to complete after IntelAGENT accepts your invitation.
</Info>

## Step 1: Log into the MOHLTC Site

Go to the [MOHLTC EDT Portal](https://www.edt.health.gov.on.ca/) and sign in using your GO Secure account.

## Step 2: Access Designee Maintenance

Click the **Designee Maintenance** link.

![Designee Maintenance](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/designee_maintenance-20512d2b4056664ba15acb626cb9e258.png)

## Step 3: Add Designee

Click **Add Designee**.

![Add Designee](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/add_designee-ab659b06baeadb808c9cb79bafc0eb71.png)

## Step 4: Enter Designee Details

Enter the following information:
- **Full Name:** IntelAGENT
- **Email:** agent@intelagent.ca

Click **Save**.

![Designee Details](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/designee_details-6624e7fc50f945c24847abb1fd07c9e5.png)

## Step 5: Confirm the Designation

Click **Confirm this Designation**.

![Confirm Designation](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/confirm_designation-a0034a424ae55c4fc6b381ca2a59e590.png)

## Step 6: Wait for Acceptance

You will receive an email from GO Secure when IntelAGENT has accepted the invitation.

## Step 7: Grant Permissions

Once you receive the acceptance email:

1. Click the link in the email to return to GO Secure
2. Click the **Designee Maintenance** link

![Maintenance](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/maintenance-20512d2b4056664ba15acb626cb9e258.png)

3. Find the row with **agent@intelagent.ca** and click on the email hyperlink

![Email Link](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/email-04b871d316476aa24fdeacc7a307c3f4.png)

4. Select the **MC EDT** hyperlink

![MC EDT Hyperlink](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/hyperlink-71a83c839cad0b49e835f6995398179b.png)

5. Click **Select All** and then **Save**

![Select All Properties](/images/docs/getting-started/assets/images/properties-683e9a4a4b1b0d8ea9b9c169eda6b8e3.png)

<Check>
IntelAGENT is now designated as your agent and can submit claims on your behalf.
</Check>
