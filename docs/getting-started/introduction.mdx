## What is IntelAGENT?

IntelAGENT is a comprehensive medical billing platform designed for Ontario healthcare providers. It simplifies the process of submitting, tracking, and managing OHIP claims through both web and mobile applications.

  <Card title="Submit Claims" icon="file-medical">

  <Card title="Track Revenue" icon="chart-line">

### Platforms

IntelAGENT is available on:

- **Web App** - Full-featured browser-based application for comprehensive claim management, reporting, and team administration
- **Mobile App** - iOS and Android apps for on-the-go claim submission with camera scanning capabilities

### Getting Started

1. **Designate IntelAGENT** - [Set up IntelAGENT as your EDT designee](/docs/getting-started/designation) with the Ministry of Health
2. **Create your account** - Sign up at [app.intelagent.ca](https://app.intelagent.ca)
3. **Download the mobile app** - Available on the App Store and Google Play
