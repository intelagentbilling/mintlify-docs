## Overview

The Charges report shows invoices for IntelAGENT services based on your subscription plan and usage.

## Accessing Charges

Navigate to **Reports** > **Charges** in the sidebar.

## Understanding Charges

IntelAGENT charges are based on your plan:

### Standard Plan
- 0.25% of paid claim amounts
- Charged monthly based on remittance advices received

### Premium Plan
- 2% of paid claim amounts
- Includes dedicated billing agent support
- Claims optimization service

### Free Plan
- No charges
- Limited features

## Invoice Details

Each invoice shows:
- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Claims processed
- Total paid amount (from Ministry)
- IntelAGENT fee
- Payment status

## Payment History

View your payment history including:
- Invoice date
- Amount charged
- Payment method used
- Receipt download

## Managing Payment Methods

To update your payment method:
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Payment Information**
3. Add or update your credit card
4. Save changes

## Billing Questions

For billing inquiries, contact [agent@intelagent.ca](mailto:agent@intelagent.ca) or use the in-app support chat.
