## Overview

The Dashboard is your central hub for understanding your billing performance at a glance. It displays key metrics, revenue trends, and recent activity.

## Revenue Metrics

The dashboard displays revenue summaries across multiple time periods:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Daily" icon="calendar-day">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Monthly" icon="calendar">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Yearly" icon="calendar-check">
    Year-to-date revenue totals
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Charts and Trends

### Revenue Chart
Visual representation of your revenue over time, showing:
- Daily/weekly/monthly trends
- Comparison with previous periods
- Projected revenue based on current patterns

### Claim Status Distribution
See the breakdown of your claims by status:
- **Submitted** - Awaiting processing
- **Partial** - Partially paid
- **Successful** - Fully paid
- **Rejected** - Requires attention

## Latest Remittance Advice

The dashboard shows your most recent remittance advice from the Ministry, including:
- Payment date
- Total amount received
- Number of claims processed

Click on the remittance advice to view full details.

## Quick Actions

From the dashboard, you can quickly:
- Create a new claim
- View recent claims
- Access reports
- Check claim status

## Accessing the Dashboard

1. Log in to [app.intelagent.ca](https://app.intelagent.ca)
2. The dashboard loads automatically for paid plan users
3. Free plan users are directed to Settings first

<Note>
Revenue data refreshes periodically. Use the refresh button to get the latest numbers.
</Note>
