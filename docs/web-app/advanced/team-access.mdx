## Overview

Team Access allows you to invite team members to help manage your billing while controlling what they can see and do.

## Accessing Team Settings

Navigate to **Settings** > **Team Access**.

## User Roles

IntelAGENT supports role-based access control:

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| Owner | Full access to all features and settings |
| Admin | Manage claims, patients, and reports; cannot manage billing or team |
| Billing | Create and submit claims; view reports |
| Viewer | Read-only access to claims and reports |

## Inviting Team Members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Access">
    Go to **Settings** > **Team Access**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Click **Invite Team Member**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Email address
    - Role
    - Facility access (optional)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Click **Send**. The invitee receives an email with instructions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Invitations

View pending invitations and:
- Resend invitation email
- Cancel invitation
- Modify role before acceptance

## Facility-Based Access

Restrict team members to specific facilities:

1. When inviting or editing a user
2. Under **Facility Access**, select specific facilities
3. User can only see claims and patients for those facilities

<Note>
Leave facility access blank to grant access to all facilities.
</Note>

## Modifying Team Members

To change a team member's access:
1. Find them in the team list
2. Click **Edit**
3. Update role or facility access
4. Save changes

## Removing Team Members

1. Find the team member
2. Click **Remove**
3. Confirm removal

<Warning>
Removed users immediately lose access. Any work in progress may be lost.
</Warning>

## Activity Logging

Track team activity:
- See who created or modified claims
- View login history
- Audit trail for compliance

## Best Practices

- **Principle of least privilege** - Grant only the access needed
- **Regular reviews** - Periodically audit team access
- **Facility restrictions** - Use for multi-location practices
- **Remove promptly** - Revoke access when team members leave
