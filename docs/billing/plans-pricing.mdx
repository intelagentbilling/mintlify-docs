## Standard Plan

The Standard plan includes everything most practices need:
- Unlimited claim submissions
- Full web and mobile access
- Revenue analytics and reports
- Team access for up to 3 users
- Excel exports

**Pricing:** 0.25% of paid claim amounts
- Based on actual payments received
- Billed monthly
- Example: $10,000 in paid claims = $25 fee

## Premium Plan

The Premium plan adds dedicated support:
- All Standard features
- Dedicated billing agent reviews your claims
- Claims optimization suggestions
- Proactive rejection management
- Unlimited team members
- Priority customer support

**Pricing:** 2% of paid claim amounts
- Includes active billing support
- Potential to recover more than the fee through optimization

## How Billing Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Claims">
    Use IntelAGENT to submit claims to OHIP
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Payments">
    OHIP processes claims and issues remittance advices
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calculate Fee">
    Your IntelAGENT fee is calculated based on paid amounts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monthly Invoice">
    Receive an invoice at the end of each billing cycle
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select your new plan
4. Enter payment information (if upgrading to paid)
5. Confirm the change

Changes take effect immediately.

## Downgrading

To downgrade:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the lower tier
4. Confirm

<Note>
Downgrading may limit access to certain features immediately.
</Note>

## Free Trial
