## Accessing Subscription Settings

Navigate to **Settings** > **Subscription** (web) or **Settings** > **Payment** (mobile).

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Payment Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Payment Settings">
    Go to Settings > Payment Information
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Card">
    Click **Add Payment Method**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Enter your credit card information:
    - Card number
    - Expiration date
    - CVC
    - Billing address
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to add the card
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Payment Information**
2. Click **Edit** on the existing card
3. Update the details
4. Save changes

### Removing Payment Method

1. Add a new payment method first
2. Click **Remove** on the old card
3. Confirm removal

<Warning>
You must have at least one valid payment method on file for paid plans.
</Warning>

## Viewing Invoices

Access your billing history:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Billing History** or **View Invoices**
3. See list of all invoices with:
   - Invoice date
   - Billing period
   - Amount
   - Payment status

### Downloading Invoices

Click on any invoice to:
- View details
- Download PDF
- Print for records

## Understanding Your Invoice

Each invoice includes:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Billing Period | The month covered |
| Claims Processed | Number of paid claims |
| Total Paid Amount | Sum of OHIP payments received |
| IntelAGENT Fee | Your plan percentage applied |
| Tax | Applicable taxes |
| Total Due | Amount charged |

## Payment Processing

- Payments are processed automatically
- Charged to your default payment method
- Typically on the 1st of each month
- Email receipt sent upon successful payment

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Update your payment method
3. Payment will retry automatically
4. Contact support if issues persist

<Note>
Service may be limited if payment remains unsuccessful after multiple attempts.
</Note>

## Cancelling Subscription

To cancel your subscription:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select a reason (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

**What happens when you cancel:**
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Data is retained for 90 days
- You can reactivate anytime
- No refunds for partial periods

## Reactivating

To reactivate after cancellation:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
3. Click **Reactivate**
4. Select a plan
5. Enter payment information
