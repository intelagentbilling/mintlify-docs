## System Requirements

### iOS
- iPhone 6s or newer
- iOS 13.0 or later
- Camera access for document scanning

### Android
- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later
- Camera access for document scanning
- 100MB free storage

## Installation Steps

### iOS

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone
2. Search for "IntelAGENT"
3. Tap **Get** to download
4. Wait for installation to complete
5. Open the app from your home screen

### Android

1. Open **Google Play Store**
2. Search for "IntelAGENT"
3. Tap **Install**
4. Accept permissions when prompted
5. Open the app once installed

## First Launch

When you first open the app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login or Sign Up">
    Enter your existing IntelAGENT credentials, or create a new account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant Permissions">
    Allow camera access for document scanning (required for full functionality)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">
    Verify your billing information and facility settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Billing">
    Navigate to the Claims tab to submit your first claim
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Login Options

### Email & Password
Enter your registered email and password.

### Magic Link
1. Tap **Sign in with Magic Link**
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox for the login link
4. Tap the link to sign in instantly

<Tip>
Magic links are great for quick, secure login without remembering passwords.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting Installation

### App won't download
- Check your internet connection
- Ensure sufficient storage space
- Try restarting the App Store / Play Store

### App crashes on launch
- Ensure your OS is updated
- Uninstall and reinstall the app
- Contact support if issues persist

### Camera not working
- Check app permissions in device settings
- Restart the app
- Ensure no other app is using the camera
