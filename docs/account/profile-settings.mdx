## Accessing Settings

### Web App
Click **Settings** in the sidebar navigation.

### Mobile App
Tap the **Settings** tab (gear icon) in the bottom navigation.

## Profile Sections

### Basic Information

Your personal account details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| First Name | Your first name |
| Last Name | Your last name |
| Email | Account email (used for login) |

To update:
1. Edit the fields
2. Click **Save**

### Personal Profile

Professional information for billing:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Billing Number | Your OHIP billing number |
| Specialty | Medical specialty |
| License Number | Professional license (optional) |

### MOH Group Settings

Ministry of Health group configuration:

- **MOH Group Number** - Your assigned group
- **Specialty Code** - OHIP specialty designation
- **Payment Program** - RMB, FFS, etc.

<Info>
MOH Group settings affect how claims are processed. Ensure accuracy.
</Info>

### Facility Associations

Manage the facilities where you practice:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Facilities**
2. View associated facilities
3. Add new facilities (if permitted by admin)
4. Set default facility

## Changing Your Email

To change your account email:

1. Go to **Settings** > **Basic Information**
2. Enter new email address
3. Click **Save**
4. Verify the new email via confirmation link

<Warning>
Your email is your login credential. Ensure you have access to the new email before changing.
</Warning>

## Changing Your Password

1. Go to **Settings** > **Change Password**
2. Enter current password
3. Enter new password
4. Confirm new password
5. Click **Save**

Password requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number

## Data Refresh

Refresh cached data from the server:

### Refresh Static Data
Updates billing codes, diagnostic codes, and fee schedules.

### Refresh Recent Data
Syncs recent claims and patient records.

### Refresh Profile
Reloads your profile information from the server.

## Help & Support

From Settings, access:

- **Ask us a question** - Opens Intercom chat
- **Documentation** - Links to this help center
- **Terms of Service** - Legal terms
- **Privacy Policy** - Data handling practices

## Deleting Your Account

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
</Warning>

To delete your account:

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Click **Delete Account**
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Confirm deletion

**What gets deleted:**
- Your account and login
- Personal profile information
- Access to the platform

**What is retained:**
- Claim records (for legal/audit requirements)
- Financial records
