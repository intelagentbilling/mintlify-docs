## Login Methods

IntelAGENT supports multiple ways to sign in:

### Email & Password

Traditional login with your credentials:
1. Go to [app.intelagent.ca/login](https://app.intelagent.ca/login)
2. Enter your email
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Sign In**

### Magic Link

Passwordless login via email:
1. Click **Sign in with Magic Link**
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Magic Link**
4. Check your email
5. Click the link to sign in instantly

<Tip>
Magic links are valid for 15 minutes and can only be used once.
</Tip>

## Creating an Account

### Sign Up Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Registration">
    Go to [app.intelagent.ca/register](https://app.intelagent.ca/register)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Email">
    Provide your email address
  </Step>

  </Step>

  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Profile">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">

  </Step>
</Steps>

## Password Recovery

### Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for the reset link
5. Click the link and enter a new password

### Reset Link Not Received?

- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Try again after a few minutes
- Contact support if issues persist

## Account Security

### Password Best Practices

- Use a unique password for IntelAGENT
- Minimum 8 characters with mixed case and numbers
- Don't share your password
- Change password periodically

### Session Management

- Sessions expire after extended inactivity
- Logging out ends your session
- Use **Log Out** when on shared devices

## Logging Out

### Web App
Click your profile icon > **Log Out**

### Mobile App
Go to **Settings** > **Log Out**

## Multiple Devices

You can be logged in on multiple devices simultaneously:
- Web browser
- Mobile app (iOS)
- Mobile app (Android)

Each device maintains its own session.

## Troubleshooting

### "Invalid credentials"
- Verify email spelling
- Check caps lock
- Try password reset

### "Account not found"
- Ensure you've registered
- Check email address
- Contact support

### "Magic link expired"
- Links expire after 15 minutes
- Request a new link
- Check email promptly

### Locked out
If you're locked out after multiple failed attempts:
- Wait 15 minutes before trying again
- Use magic link login
- Contact support for assistance
