## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Designate IntelAGENT">
    [Set up IntelAGENT as your EDT designee](/docs/getting-started/designation) with the Ministry of Health.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
    Sign up at [app.intelagent.ca](https://app.intelagent.ca/register) with your billing number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Billing">
    Submit your first claim via web or download the mobile app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
